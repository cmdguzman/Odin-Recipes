Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

Grilled cheese sandwich in a nonstick pan with buttered bread and American Cheddar for a classic hot sandwich.


Ingredients:

  • 4 slices white bread
  • 3 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 2 slices Cheddar cheese

Steps:

  1. Preheat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Generously butter one side
    of a slice of bread. Place bread butter-side down in the hot skillet;
    add 1 slice of cheese. Butter a second slice of bread on one side and
    place butter-side up on top of cheese.
  2. Cook until lightly browned on one side; flip over and continue cooking
    until cheese is melted. Repeat with remaining 2 slices of bread, butter,
    and slice of cheese.
